Welcome to Hangbot!

Game Rules

  • This is a word guessing game, in which you must decipher a secret word randomly generated by the computer, one letter at a time;
  • You can choose the game mode: take your time and play with no time limit, or use the timer and challenge yourself to find all the letters in 30 seconds;
  • You only get 7 incorrect guesses;
  • Each incorrect guess takes our robot closer to be hanged and ads a new part to the 'hangbot';
  • If you need help, for each word you can click the Hint button(or press Alt+H on your keyboard) and you will get a definition of that word;
  • Good luck!
