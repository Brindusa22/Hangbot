Welcome to Hangbot!

Game Rules

  • This is a computer-tech term quiz, in which you must decipher a secret word, randomly generated by the computer, one letter at a time;
  • You can choose the game mode: take your time and play with no time limit, or use the timer and challenge yourself to find all the letters in 45 seconds;
  • Each incorrect guess takes our robot closer to be hanged by adding a new part to the 'hangbot'. If you make seven mistakes, you lose;
  • If you need a hint, for each word you can click the button (or press Alt+H on your keyboard) and you will get a definition of that word from the TechTerms.com dictionary.

Good luck and enjoy!
